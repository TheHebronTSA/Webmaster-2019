Design Philosophy

IBM Plex Sans
IBM Plex Sans Condensed

Bold. New. Refreshing. This is Hebron TSA. The same old Google Fonts and Material Design colors seemed boring to us. In an effort to stand out from other TSA Webmaster projects, we wanted to try something fresh but classy, taking inspiration from the colors of printer ink cartridges and the font from old IBM computers to combine old with new.
