Community Service

The Big Event

Every year, the Hebron Technology Student Association participates in the local LISD West Zone Big Event, an event where organizations from around the Lewisville Independent School District conglomerate and participate in a day of service to help elderly homes, hospitals, churches, and many more. Last year, Hebron TSA helped an elderly lady with housekeeping chores such as cleaning the roof.

Operation Kindness Charity Drive

Every year Hebron TSA participates in the local Operation Kindness Charity drive. Members of our chapter bring items that help sustain the nonprofit dog shelter and keep the animals healthy. The friendly competition between the underclassmen and upperclassmen helps toward fulfilling the animals needs.
